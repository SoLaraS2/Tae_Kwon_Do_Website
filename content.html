Tae Gukks

Tae Gukk Il-Jang

  1. Charyeot, Kyeongrye (attention, bow)
  2. Junbi (ready stance)
  3. Turn 90° left into Ap Kubi, Arae Makki (low block)
  4. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi (middle punch)
  5. Turn 180° right into Ap Kubi, Arae Makki
  6. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  7. Turn 90° left into Ap Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki (outward block)
  8. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  9. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  10. Turn 270° left into Ap Kubi, Arae Makki
  11. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  12. Turn 180° into Ap Kubi, Arae Makki
  13. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  14. Turn 90° left into Ap Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  15. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  16. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  17. Turn 270° left into Ap Kubi, Arae Makki
  18. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  19. Turn 180° into Ap Kubi, Arae Makki
  20. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  21. Return to Junbi (ready stance)

Tae Gukk Ii-Jang

  1. Charyeot, Kyeongrye
  2. Junbi
  3. Turn 90° left into Ap Kubi, Momtong An Makki (inward middle block)
  4. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  5. Turn 180° right into Ap Kubi, Momtong An Makki
  6. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  7. Turn 90° left into Ap Seogi, Momtong Bakat Makki (outward block)
  8. Step forward Ap Seogi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  9. Turn 90° left into Ap Kubi, Arae Makki (low block)
  10. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  11. Turn 180° into Ap Kubi, Arae Makki
  12. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  13. Turn 90° left into Ap Seogi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  14. Step forward Ap Seogi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  15. Turn 90° left into Ap Kubi, Eolgul Makki (high block)
  16. Step forward Ap Kubi, Eolgul Jireugi (high punch)
  17. Turn 180° into Ap Kubi, Eolgul Makki
  18. Step forward Ap Kubi, Eolgul Jireugi
  19. Return to Junbi

Tae Gukk Sam-Jang

  1. Charyeot, Kyeongrye
  2. Junbi
  3. Turn 90° left, Ap Kubi, Arae Makki
  4. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  5. Turn 180° right, Ap Kubi, Arae Makki
  6. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  7. Turn 90° left, Ap Seogi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  8. Ap Cha Olligi (front rising kick), land Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  9. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  10. Turn 270° left, Ap Kubi, Arae Makki
  11. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  12. Turn 180°, Ap Kubi, Arae Makki
  13. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  14. Turn 90° left, Dwi Kubi, Momtong An Makki
  15. Turn 90° right, Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  16. Turn 180°, Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  17. Turn 90° left, Dwi Kubi, Momtong An Makki
  18. Turn 90° right, Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  19. Turn 180°, Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  20. Turn 90° left, Ap Seogi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  21. Return to Junbi

Tae Gukk Sah-Jang

  1. Charyeot, Kyeongrye
  2. Junbi
  3. Turn 90° left into Dwi Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  4. Move forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  5. Turn 180° right into Dwi Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  6. Move forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  7. Turn 90° left into Ap Seogi, Arae Makki
  8. Step forward Ap Seogi, Momtong Jireugi
  9. Turn 90° left into Ap Kubi, Eolgul Makki (high block)
  10. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  11. Turn 270° left into Ap Kubi, Arae Makki
  12. Move forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  13. Turn 180° into Ap Kubi, Arae Makki
  14. Move forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  15. Turn 90° left into Dwi Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  16. Move forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  17. Turn 180° right into Dwi Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  18. Move forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  19. Turn 90° left into Ap Seogi, Arae Makki
  20. Step forward Ap Seogi, Momtong Jireugi
  21. Return to Junbi

Tae Gukk Oo-Jang

  1. Charyeot, Kyeongrye
  2. Junbi
  3. Turn 90° left into Ap Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki (outward block)
  4. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  5. Turn 180° right into Ap Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  6. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  7. Turn 90° left into Dwi Kubi, Eolgul Makki (high block)
  8. Shift to Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  9. Turn 90° left into Ap Seogi, Arae Makki (low block)
  10. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  11. Turn 270° left into Ap Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  12. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  13. Turn 180° into Ap Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  14. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  15. Turn 90° left into Dwi Kubi, Eolgul Makki
  16. Shift to Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  17. Turn 90° left into Ap Seogi, Arae Makki
  18. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  19. Turn 180° Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  20. Turn 180° again (facing front), Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi (optional in some schools)
  21. Return to Junbi

Tae Gukk Chil-Jang

  1. Charyeot, Kyeongrye
  2. Junbi
  3. Turn 90° left into Dwi Kubi, Batangson Momtong Makki (palm block)
  4. Shift to Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  5. Turn 180° right into Dwi Kubi, Batangson Momtong Makki
  6. Shift to Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  7. Turn 90° left into Ap Seogi, Arae Makki
  8. Step forward Ap Seogi, Momtong Jireugi
  9. Turn 90° left into Ap Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  10. Ap Cha Olligi, land Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  11. Turn 270° left into Dwi Kubi, Momtong Makki
  12. Shift to Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  13. Turn 180° into Dwi Kubi, Momtong Makki
  14. Shift to Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  15. Turn 90° left into Dwi Kubi, Sonnal Makki (knife-hand block)
  16. Step forward Ap Kubi, Sonnal An Chigi (knife-hand inward strike)
  17. Turn 180° right into Dwi Kubi, Sonnal Makki
  18. Step forward Ap Kubi, Sonnal An Chigi
  19. Turn 90° left into Ap Seogi, Arae Makki
  20. Step forward Ap Seogi, Momtong Jireugi
  21. Turn 90° left, Dwi Kubi, Momtong Makki
  22. Turn 180° right, Dwi Kubi, Momtong Makki
  23. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  24. Turn 90° left, Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  25. Turn 180° left, Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  26. Return to Junbi

Tae Gukk Pal-Jang

  1. Charyeot, Kyeongrye
  2. Junbi
  3. Turn 90° left, Dwi Kubi, Sonnal Momtong Makki (knife-hand block)
  4. Shift to Ap Kubi, Sonnal An Chigi (knife-hand inward strike)
  5. Turn 180° right, Dwi Kubi, Sonnal Momtong Makki
  6. Shift to Ap Kubi, Sonnal An Chigi
  7. Turn 90° left, Ap Kubi, Arae Makki (low block)
  8. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  9. Turn 90° left, Ap Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki (outward block)
  10. Dollyeo Chagi (roundhouse kick), land Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  11. Turn 270° left into Dwi Kubi, Eolgul Makki (high block)
  12. Shift to Ap Kubi, Eolgul Jireugi (high punch)
  13. Turn 180°, Dwi Kubi, Eolgul Makki
  14. Shift to Ap Kubi, Eolgul Jireugi
  15. Turn 90° left, Ap Seogi, Arae Makki
  16. Step forward Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  17. Turn 180°, Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  18. Turn 90° right, Ap Kubi, Momtong Bakat Makki
  19. Ap Cha Olligi, land Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  20. Turn 270° right, Dwi Kubi, Sonnal Makki
  21. Shift to Ap Kubi, Sonnal An Chigi
  22. Turn 180° left, Dwi Kubi, Sonnal Makki
  23. Shift to Ap Kubi, Sonnal An Chigi
  24. Turn 90° left, Ap Kubi, Momtong Jireugi
  25. Return to Junbi

Our Philosophy

Foundational principles that guide our Tae Kwon Do practice. These core values inspire every movement, every step, and every punch. Our key wonshims include:

  • Integrity: Doing the right thing, even when no one is watching.
  • Perseverance: Overcoming challenges and pushing forward.
  • Self-Control: Mastering your impulses to remain calm and focused.
  • Indomitable Spirit: Displaying courage in the face of adversity.
